Common questions

How do I restore a backup?

Open Settings in the app, choose your backend, then use "Restore from Backup". This replaces local data.

Can I export my records?

Yes. WrenchBook supports local ZIP backup and PDF report exports for vehicle history.

Is location required?

No. Location for fuel entries is optional and can be disabled in iOS settings.
