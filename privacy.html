Privacy Policy for WrenchBook

Effective date: February 25, 2026

WrenchBook ("we," "our," or "us") provides an iOS app for vehicle fuel and maintenance tracking. This policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information You Add

You may add and store:

  • Vehicle details (for example make, model, year, mileage)
  • Fuel records (amount, cost, odometer, optional location)
  • Maintenance records (category, cost, notes, mileage)
  • Receipt photos and OCR text generated from those photos

2. Permissions

WrenchBook can request the following permissions when you use related features:

  • Camera: capture receipt photos
  • Photo Library: attach existing receipt images
  • Location (optional): store fill-up location on your request

3. Data Storage

  • Primary app data is stored locally on your device.
  • If you enable cloud backup, backup files are stored in accounts you control (for example Google Drive or iCloud, depending on configuration).
  • We do not sell your personal data.

4. Backup and Third-Party Services

Optional backup features can use:

  • Google Sign-In and Google Drive API
  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit services

Those services are governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or broker your data. Information is only shared when:

  • You explicitly enable backup or export behavior, or
  • Required by law.

6. Data Retention

  • Local data stays on your device until removed by you.
  • Backup data remains in your chosen cloud provider until you delete it.

7. Your Controls

  • Disable permissions in iOS settings at any time
  • Stop using cloud backup in app settings
  • Delete records or uninstall the app

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards, but no storage or transmission method is 100% secure.

9. Children

WrenchBook is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated over time. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact contact@friendlyit.xyz.
