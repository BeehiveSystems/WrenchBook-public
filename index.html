Privacy Policy for WrenchBook

Effective date: February 22, 2026

WrenchBook ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the WrenchBook mobile app ("App") collects, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Vehicle details (for example: make, model, year, mileage)
  • Maintenance and fuel log entries
  • Receipt photos and related notes
  • Optional account data used for Google Drive backup/sign-in

Information from device permissions

  • Camera: used to capture receipt photos.
  • Location (optional): used to save fuel fill-up location when you choose to enable it.
  • Photo library (optional): used if you select images from your library.

Automatically collected information

  • Basic app diagnostics/crash data (if enabled by Apple system services)
  • We do not collect unnecessary personal information for advertising.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (maintenance and fuel tracking)
  • Store and organize your records
  • Enable optional backup/restore with Google Drive
  • Improve reliability and fix issues

3. Data Storage and Backup

  • Your data is stored locally on your device.
  • If you choose Google Drive backup, backup files are uploaded to your Google account under your control.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

WrenchBook may use:

  • Google Sign-In / Google Drive API for optional backup and restore
  • Apple platform services (for distribution, diagnostics, and app operation)

Use of third-party services is subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data when:

  • You explicitly enable a feature (such as Google Drive backup), or
  • Required by law.

6. Data Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud backup data remains in your Google Drive until you remove it.

7. Your Choices

  • Disable location/camera/photo access in iOS Settings
  • Stop using Google Drive backup at any time
  • Delete app data by removing entries or uninstalling the app

8. Children's Privacy

WrenchBook is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

10. International Users

If you use the App outside your home country, you understand your information may be processed in countries where the services operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy with a revised effective date.
